**Comparing Dynamics: Deep Neural Networks versus Glassy Systems**

M. Baity-Jesi, L. Sagun, M. Geiger, S. Spigler, G. Ben Arous, C. Cammarota, Y. LeCun, M. Wyart, G. Biroli

Presented at ICML 2018, in Stockholm.
